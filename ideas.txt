Physics for Actor2D
--------------------
Add RigidBody2D as invisible child of Actor2D. The RigidBody2D simulates physics
and Actor2D syncs its position in _physics_process:

    @onready var physics_body: RigidBody2D = $RigidBody2D

    func _physics_process(delta):
        if physics_body:
            global_position = physics_body.global_position

Wrap physics calls in simple helpers:

    func impulse(force: Vector2):
        if physics_body:
            physics_body.apply_central_impulse(force)

    func push(force: Vector2):
        if physics_body:
            physics_body.apply_central_force(force)

Physics becomes optional - actors without the child node just ignore these calls.
